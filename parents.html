Welcome Parents!

Here you will find ways to support your child in his or her journey in applying to college!



Financial Aid

  1. In order to receive Federal Financial Aid, Parents should make a FAFSA account to create a FAFSA ID
  2. After your child completes the FAFSA, encourage them to apply for New York State Financial Aid if they are attending a school in New York State by making a NYSTAP account.
  3. Encourage your child to Apply For Scholarships by accessing the HSTAT shared Google Drive Folder
